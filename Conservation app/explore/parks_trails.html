New Zealand's Parks & Trails

Discover Aotearoa's National Parks and Scenic Trails

New Zealand is world-renowned for its stunning natural landscapes, which are meticulously preserved within a network of national parks and an extensive system of walking and hiking trails (locally known as tramps). These areas offer unparalleled opportunities to experience pristine wilderness, diverse ecosystems, and breathtaking scenery.

From towering mountain ranges and ancient rainforests to dramatic coastlines and crystal-clear lakes, New Zealand's parks and trails cater to all levels of adventurers, from short nature walks to multi-day Great Walks.
